VocabDeck — Free K-12 Vocabulary Flashcards

English, Math & Science Flashcards for Every Grade Level

VocabDeck is a free online flashcard app designed for students in Kindergarten through 12th grade. With over 4,200 vocabulary terms across three subjects — English, Math, and Science — students can study the words they need to succeed in school.

English Vocabulary (1,970+ words)

Grade-appropriate English vocabulary words with clear, student-friendly definitions. From basic Kindergarten words like "bloom" and "gentle" to advanced Grade 12 terms like "juxtaposition" and "paradigm." Perfect for building reading comprehension and preparing for standardized tests.

Math Vocabulary (840+ terms)

Complete math terminology from counting and shapes in Kindergarten to calculus concepts in Grade 12. Includes formulas for area, perimeter, volume, circumference, and surface area. Features SVG shape illustrations for geometry terms including triangles, rectangles, parallelograms, trapezoids, circles, cubes, cylinders, cones, spheres, parallel lines, perpendicular lines, and angles. Aligned with Georgia K-12 Mathematics Standards (2021).

Science Vocabulary (1,480+ terms)

Comprehensive science vocabulary aligned with Georgia Standards of Excellence and Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS). Covers Earth Science, Life Science, and Physical Science at every grade level. From weather and seasons in Kindergarten to physics and chemistry in Grade 12. Includes topics tested on Georgia Milestones: cells, ecosystems, forces, energy, atoms, genetics, and more.

Features

Shuffle or alphabetical card ordering. Swipe or tap navigation. Keyboard shortcuts for desktop. Progress tracking. Works offline as a Progressive Web App (PWA). No login, no ads, completely free. Georgia Milestones test prep vocabulary.
