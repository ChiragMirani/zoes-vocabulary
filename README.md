# VocabDeck — Free K-12 Vocabulary Flashcards

**[Use VocabDeck Now](https://chiragmirani.github.io/zoes-vocabulary/)**

VocabDeck is a free flashcard app with **4,200+ vocabulary words** for students in Kindergarten through 12th grade. Three subjects, every grade level, no login required.

## Subjects

### English Vocabulary (1,970+ words)
Grade-appropriate vocabulary with student-friendly definitions. From basic Kindergarten words to advanced Grade 12 academic terms.

### Math Vocabulary (840+ terms)
Complete math terminology with **formulas** for area, perimeter, volume, circumference, and surface area. Includes **SVG shape illustrations** for geometry terms — triangles, parallelograms, circles, cubes, parallel lines, angles, and more. Aligned with **Georgia K-12 Mathematics Standards (2021)**.

### Science Vocabulary (1,480+ terms)
Earth Science, Life Science, and Physical Science at every grade level. From weather and seasons in Kindergarten to physics and chemistry in Grade 12. Aligned with **Georgia Standards of Excellence (GSE)** and **Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS)**.

## Features

- **4,200+ flashcards** across English, Math, and Science
- **Grades K–12** with grade-specific word lists
- **SVG illustrations** for geometry shapes and science concepts
- **Math formulas** included in definitions
- **Shuffle or A–Z** card ordering
- **Swipe, tap, or keyboard** navigation
- **Progress bar** showing position in the deck
- **Share link** to copy a direct URL to any subject/grade
- **Works offline** as a Progressive Web App (PWA)
- **No login, no ads, completely free**

## Standards Alignment

- Georgia Standards of Excellence (GSE) for Math and Science
- Georgia Milestones Assessment System vocabulary
- Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS)
- Tier 2 academic vocabulary for English Language Arts

## Georgia Milestones Test Prep

VocabDeck's vocabulary is aligned with the standards tested on the Georgia Milestones EOG (End of Grade) and EOC (End of Course) assessments. Students preparing for Milestones in grades 3–8 and high school can use VocabDeck to review key terms in Math, Science, and ELA.

## How to Use

1. Visit [chiragmirani.github.io/zoes-vocabulary](https://chiragmirani.github.io/zoes-vocabulary/)
2. Select a subject (English, Math, or Science)
3. Choose your grade level from the dropdown
4. Tap cards to reveal definitions
5. Swipe left/right or use arrow keys to navigate

## Install as App

On mobile: Open the site in Safari or Chrome, then "Add to Home Screen" to install VocabDeck as a standalone app that works offline.

## Also Available on the App Store

VocabDeck is also available as a native iOS app: [VocabDeck on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/vocabdeck)

## Tech Stack

Single HTML file, vanilla JavaScript, no frameworks. Progressive Web App with service worker for offline support. SVG illustrations rendered inline. All data embedded — no API calls, no backend.
